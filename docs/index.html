About

moneytweet is a system developed by researchers at the Hamburg University of Applied Sciences. The goal is to track the real-time trends of a specific financial subject and compare the sentiment to the live stocks.

The goal is to give a prediction for stock markets to support inexperienced traders. By using the moneytweet system it is possible to estimate the future stock development based on historic events.

Combining artificial intelligence and big data is the key of our system. Data is being collected, analyzed and used for precise predictions. Our system implements utilizes the big data architecture Lambda to provide fast access and computation power for the use case of our system.

Real-time money.

Managing money has never been easy. Therefore, choosing the right financial products is very important. However, there is a specific risk when it comes to buying stocks.
moneytweet is able to provide the necessary support in topic. Not only does it combine historical trends but also estimates future stock trends and detects sudden changes. This makes it a reliable system to gather information about the status of the stock market and favorite financial products.

Expert advice

Talking about finances can sometimes be a sensitive topic. Especially when talking to strangers. moneytweet supports you in your financial situation without having to give any information about yourself. As this provided service is free to use, you so not have to concern about your private data at all. However, moneytweet does a great job guiding you in any decicions with your money.

Real-time trends.

Stock markets do also impact other areas. moneytweet analyzes real-time trends of stock related topics to generate correlations improve its predictions. This can be used to evaluate historic evenets as well as getting live updates of possible stock market developments in the near future.
